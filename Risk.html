The Risk boardgame dice roll probability calculator and battle simulation

This application will help you understand the mathematics and statistics behind Risk dice throws. You can use it to calculate general probabilities or simulate specific battle situations. The simulator will use 10.000 fights (roughly 50.000 dice rolls) to determine the outcome.
